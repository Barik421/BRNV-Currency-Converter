Privacy

Privacy Policy

BRNV Currency Converter is a static currency conversion website. It does not require an account and does not ask users to submit personal financial information.

Local preferences

The site may use your browser's localStorage to remember simple preferences, such as selected currencies, theme, and language. This data stays in your browser and is used only to improve the experience on future visits.

Exchange rates

The converter requests exchange-rate data from a public currency API directly from your browser. Rates are provided for informational purposes only and may not reflect final bank, broker, or payment-provider rates.

Analytics and advertising

The site includes placeholder markup for Google Analytics 4 and Google AdSense. If real IDs are added later, Google services may collect usage and advertising data according to Google's own policies.
